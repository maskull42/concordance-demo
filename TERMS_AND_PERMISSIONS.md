# Terms and Permissions Record

## Project use

Concordance makes bounded comparative calls to a declared panel of AI models, stores the returned text as cached audit records, and displays those records with provider/model attribution. It does not provide model access to site visitors, train a competing model, or make live provider calls from the deployed site.

## Author confirmation

A.G. Elrod has confirmed that the permissions applicable to his accounts authorize the planned comparative use of the eight approved model families, including Cohere and xAI, and the public display of their cached outputs in this prototype.

This record documents the project owner's confirmation. It does not claim that public provider terms alone grant every right, and it does not publish any private agreement.

## Release safeguards

- Exact provider and model identifiers are displayed with each output.
- AI-generated text is labeled as AI-generated and is never represented as human-authored.
- Provider marks remain the property of their owners.
- Outputs are not placed under the repository's MIT or CC BY licenses.
- No output is used as the sole source of factual or scholarly claims.
- The project owner reviews current applicable terms before the public release.
